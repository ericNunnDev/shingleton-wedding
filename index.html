Our Story

Our love story began back in January, 2013. While Jacqueline was attending Central Michigan University, she was introduced to Timothy through his sister, Alexandrea. It took very little time for them to recognize a strong connection between each other. The night they met was all that it took, and the rest is history.


Years of memories later, and the bride and groom's relationship has endured many triumphs and tribulations. The greatest highlights include the birth of their beautiful daughters, Charlotte and Violette. As well as a new home, with 3 fur babies Prince, Maci, and Maverick. They cannot wait to make official what they have known all along: that they are made for each other.
